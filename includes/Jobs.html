  • Position: Research Scientists
  • Organization: Beihang University and University of Edinburgh
  • Department: International Research Center on Big Data at Beihang University

The newly established International Research Center on Big Data at Beihang University is recruiting two research scientists, joint with the Database Group at the University of Edinburgh.

We invite outstanding candidates with interests in topics in connection with big data, from theory to systems, to apply. While the positions are primarily open to database researchers with a promising academic record, all applicants with a strong background in computer science will be considered. Skills in data management, logic, automata, algorithms, complexity theory, as well as systems building experience, are particularly valuable.

Interested candidates are encouraged to contact Wenfei Fan: wenfei@inf.ed.ac.uk, University of Edinburgh and Beihang University, or Shuai Ma: mashuai@act.buaa.edu.cn, Beihang University

Beihang University is one of the top-ranked computer science departments in China. It has recently established the International Research Center on Big Data, and has committed to building up its strength in data management. The Database Group at the University of Edinburgh is one of the leading research groups in the world. There are several ongoing joint research projects between Beihang and Edinburgh, in connection with big data. Researchers are expected to work in the International Research Center at Beihang, in Beijing, China, but may take advantage of an agreement between Beihang and Edinburgh and spend up to six months each year in Edinburgh, UK, working with people in the Database Group at Edinburgh.

  • Term : 3 years.
  • Salary Scale : USD $45,000 - $55,000 pa
  • Type of position : Research Scientist
  • Degree required : PhD
  • Years of experience : 0
  • Citizenship requirements : No restriction
  • Job start date : April 2013
  • Application Deadline : 2013-03-15
  • Job location : Beijing, China, and/or Edinburgh, UK

For more information about the International Research Center at Beihang and the Database Group at Edinburgh, please visit The URL of the center page http://www.lfcs.inf.ed.ac.uk/research/database/Members.html

Aiming at building an international-recognized academic intellectual nexus to lead the research and development in field of the Big Data Science & Engineering, International Research Center on Big Data (RCBD) will carry out world-class fundamental research and foster extensive cooperation with high ranked universities and industrial partners.
